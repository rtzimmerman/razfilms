Rise of the Valiant

Rise of the Valiant is a documentary film about the World War II battle at Okinawa which was the last and bloodiest battle of that war. The film includes interviews from veterans of the Sixth Marine Division who relive their frontline experiences. Also interviewed is military historian and author Bill Sloan. The film is filled with pictures and footage from that battle. Rise of the Valiant won the 2015 Marine Corps Heritage Foundation Norman Hatch Award for best documentary films."
